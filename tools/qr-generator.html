What is a QR Code?

A Quick Response (QR) code is a type of matrix barcode first designed in 1994 for the automotive industry in Japan. A QR code uses four standardized encoding modes (numeric, alphanumeric, byte/binary, and kanji) to store data efficiently.

How to use this QR Generator?

  1. Type or paste your URL or text into the input field above.
  2. Click the "Generate QR Code" button.
  3. The tool will instantly create a unique QR code for you.
  4. Click "Download PNG" to save the code to your device.

Benefits of using QR Codes

  • Fast Access: Users can scan and visit sites in seconds.
  • Versatility: Use them on business cards, posters, or menus.
  • Touchless: Perfect for a post-pandemic world.
